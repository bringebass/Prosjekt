About The Website Central

Our mission

Ever struggled to find the right web hosting service that suits you best?

The website central is a dedicated team that compares and evaluates different types of web hosting providers. Our mission is to evaluate the top web hosting providers, giving you insight into the pros and cons of their services. We are dedicated to giving you a balanced evaluation of what web hosting providers are best for your exact needs.

We have seen the increase in demand for web hosting services over the years. Now finally, we have created the tools needed to decipher what is essential, and what is not, for people who are looking to host their own web page. We now present these tools to choose the correct web hosting provider and type to use. We finance this website through commissions from the different webhosting providers.

The Team

The Website Central is a project driven by web hosting enthusiasts, sharing information regarding web hosting companies we believe are the best in the business. The site was founded and run by Oyvind and Markus, and has several other contributors related to content creation and translation across 3 continents.

Disclaimer

Thewebsitecentral.com provides professional web services reviews fully independent of any other entity. Our assessments of the service providers are fair and balanced, and we only recommend service providers that we believe provide the most value to our readers. While compensation is sometimes received from some of the companies listed on this site (when links are used to make a purchase), service providers can not pay to change or eliminate our reviews.

Thewebsitecentral.com is regularly updating the reviews, but are not always informed by the service providers of changes in their services and can therefore not guarantee that the information is 100% accurate at any given time. Therefore, all readers must always consult the service provider’s web pages for the most updated information regarding their respective service offerings.
