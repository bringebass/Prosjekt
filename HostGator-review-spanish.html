Introducción

Resumen

En general, HostGator es asequible, con una funcionalidad y escalabilidad decentes.

Tenga en cuenta que

Nuestra evaluación de HostGator está destinada a particulares y pequeñas y medianas empresas. Dicho esto, aunque no cubrimos este segmento en nuestra evaluación, HostGator también brinda servicios a clientes más grandes con servidores dedicados o soluciones en la nube.

Inormación de la Empresa

HostGator fue fundado en 2002 y se ha convertido en uno de los 10 nombres más grandes y conocidos de la industria del alojamiento. Es una de las 10 empresas de alojamiento web más grandes del mundo, con más de 8.000.000 de dominios alojados. La empresa tiene su sede en Huston, Texas (EE. UU.) Y cuenta con más de 850 empleados. La empresa fue vendida por su fundador en 2012 y actualmente es propiedad de Endurance International Group, que también posee marcas de alojamiento conocidas como BlueHost, FatCow y domain.com.

Planes

La compañía proporciona alojamiento web compartido, para revendedor, servidor privado virtual y dedicado a consumidores individuales, pequeñas y grandes empresas (en particular, tiene algunos clientes de Fortune500). En general, la diferencia en los planes difiere en términos de precio, rendimiento, funcionalidad y debería brindar opciones para cualquier persona, desde personas que crean un blog hasta pequeñas empresas que comienzan con el comercio electrónico o grandes empresas con una gran red de sitios y subdominios.

Precio

Storage / CPU

HostGator describes storage capacity and CPU use as “unlimited” for its standard shared hosting plans - which is only within limits. GostGator states in their terms: “-we do require all customers to be fully compliant with our Terms of Service and to only utilize disk space and bandwidth in the normal operation of a personal or small business website”.

The normal operation limits of disk space and bandwidth is not specified on their web site, but customer support tells me it should hold 7,000 - 8,000 visitors / day. This limit should be adequate for the large majority of personal and small business sites. If your site were close to exceed these undisclosed limits you will receive an email “asking you to reduce usage” or the website will be suspended (temporarily stopped).

If higher performance is needed, you easily can upgrade to a fully managed VPS plan or dedicated server, which require 24 to 48 hour of migration, but is easy and more scalable than the shared hosting plans. If you are considering using the HostGator hosting plan - this page will tell you how many visitors you can expect to support (note that the stats are based on the average web page): Are There Traffic Limits on My Website?

Bandwith

HostGator describes the plans storage capacity and CPU use as “unmetered” (unlimited) for the shared hosting plans. However, similar to “Storage / _CPU” above, this is within the limits expected of normal operations, which should be adequate for the vast majority of blogs and small commercial sites. If you are approaching this limit, you will get a notification and will be able to upgrade to a larger plan.

Scalability

In case you receive more visitors to your site than what you originally estimated, and are in jeopardy of reaching the monthly limit (e.g. for bandwidth or storage), then HostGator enables you to upgrade your plan at the click of a button in cPanel. This is a needed functionality if you’re very uncertain of the likely.

Stability

All shared web hosting plans include a 45-day money-back and 99.9% uptime guarantees, which is great although quite standard among serious hosting vendors.

User-Friendliness

cPanel is standard for HostGator, and is a convenient interface to govern many aspects of your website.

The quickInstall tool, available on all web hosting plans, you can create many types of website: blog, forum, CMS, wiki, photo gallery, E-commerce store, and so much more.

Functionalities

HostGator provides standard functionalities for their cheapest plans. These are one click WordPress installs, free SSL certificate, free Domain

Hostgator also provides a website builder with 100s of templates, which makes it easy to set up the foundational structure of the page, freeing up more time for content creation. HostGator has free migration support in case you like to move your site from another hosting provider.

In sum, the amount and quality of functionalities are good, although not extraordinary.

Support

Availability: The company provides 24/7/365 support by telephone, live chat and e-mail for all enquiries which is market leading

Language: HostGator has support in English, Spanish, Portugese, Chinese, Turkish, Indonesean

Test: We have tested the only support chat, with some in-depth questions. After we posted our question it took 2 minutes for the customer advisor to be connected. After that it took 4 minutes for the advisor to come back with a relevant answer - which seems to be quite adequate.

It's worth stating that the advisor and myself got disconnected due to a glitch in their system, however, another advisor was immediately allocated and picked up the thread with my followup questions.

For most of the questions, the advisor gave fast adequate answers - however, for one of the more technical questions it took about 30 mins for them to come back with an answer. Therefore, we will not give.

Pros and cons

Pros

  • Price (on 12+ months sign up) is among the lowest in the industry
  • 24/7 support in many languages is industry leading access
  • Easy website builder enables you to set up your website fast and intuitive way
  • Many website templates
  • 45 days money back guarantee
  • Free SSL certificate

Cons

As mentioned earlier, there are drawbacks to every hosting service on this planet. Here are the ones concerning HostGator:

  • Low pricing is only promotional. That means if you dont want to sign up for 12 months or more, then the price will be higher, although still amonth the lower in the industry.
  • You do not get a free domain when signing up for HostGators services. That stated, it is quite normal to provide this for a fee.

Conclusion

HostGator is an allround great service-provider, and delivers one of the best value for money in the business (if you are able to sign up for a plan and commit for a longer time). We belive you will have access to a wide range of essential features, a fully developmed web site builder with many templates, and at the same time pay a fairly low price.
