FastComet Hosting Plans and Prices

FastComet offers three main shared hosting plans: StartSmart, ScaleRight and SpeedUp.

The StartSmart plan: costs $2.95 per month, which is the cheapest among FastComet hosting. The plan supports a (free) website and provides 15GB of SSD storage. It is recommended to receive websites with up to 25,000 visitors per month.

The ScaleRight hosting plan: allows users to host an unlimited number of websites, has 25GB of SSD storage capacity, and can back up up to 7 times a day. This plan is recommended for websites with up to 50,000 visits per month.

The SpeedU Hosting Plan: is the most expensive shared hosting plan on FastComet. The price of the plan starts at $9.95 and has first-class features, including support for an unlimited number of websites, up to 35GB of SSD storage and 6GB of RAM. This plan is recommended for a website that receives approximately 100,000 visitors per month.
