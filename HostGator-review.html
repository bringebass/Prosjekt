HostGator review

Rating:

4/5 stars

Price

24/7 customer support

Reliable servers

User friendly

No demo version

Try HostGator
Intro

Our assessment of HostGator is meant for private individuals and small to medium sized businesses. That said, even though we do not cover this segment in our assessment, Hostgator also serves larger clients with dedicated servers or cloud solutions.

Company info

HostGator was founded in 2002 and has become one of the 10 largest and most known names in the hosting industry. is one of the world's top 10 largest web hosting companies, with over 8,000,000 hosted domains. The company is based in Huston, Texas (US) and has over 850 employees. The company was sold by its founder in 2012, and is currently owned by Endurance International Group, which also owns known hosting brands as BlueHost, FatCow, and domain.com

Plans

HostGator offers the lowest promotion prices we have seen, however, you’ll have to commit to a plan for 12 months to receive these low rates (generally 60% off). That stated, the renewal rates you’ll have to pay afterwards are among the lowest that we have identified. Please note that the promotional prices do not include VAT. In addition, HostGator throws in 100 USD for both Google Ads and Bing Ads (NB! this requires you to spend 25 USD with Google before claiming the credit, and it is a requirement that you have not already signed up a google Adwords account before) Although HostGator’s more expensive plans (mostly for larger business needs) are priced closer to other competitors, the cheaper and mid-range plans are generally among the most affordable in the market. Another upside for these plans are that you don't need to sign a contract, which means you can cancel the service without extra charge.

Performance
Storage / CPU

HostGator describes storage capacity and CPU use as “unlimited” for its standard shared hosting plans - which is only within limits. GostGator states in their terms: “-we do require all customers to be fully compliant with our Terms of Service and to only utilize disk space and bandwidth in the normal operation of a personal or small business website”. The normal operation limits of disk space and bandwidth is not specified on their web site, but customer support tells me it should hold 7,000 - 8,000 visitors / day. This limit should be adequate for the large majority of personal and small business sites. If your site were close to exceed these undisclosed limits you will receive an email “asking you to reduce usage” or the website will be suspended (temporarily stopped). If more performance is needed, you easily can upgrade to a fully managed VPS plan or dedicated server, which require 24 to 48 hour of migration, but is easy and more scalable than the shared hosting plans. If you are considering using the HostGator hosting plan - this page will tell you how many visitors you can expect to support (note that the stats are based on the average web page): Are There Traffic Limits on My Website?

Bandwith

HostGator describes the plans storage capacity and CPU use as “unmetered” (unlimited) for the shared hosting plans. However, similar to “Storage / _CPU” above, this is within the limits expected of normal operations, which should be adequate for the vast majority of blogs and small commercial sites. If you are approaching this limit, you will get a notification and will be able to upgrade to a larger plan.

Scalability

In case you receive more visitors to your site than what you originally estimated, and are in jeopardy of reaching the monthly limit (e.g. for bandwidth or storage), then HostGator enables you to upgrade your plan at the click of a button in cPanel. This is a needed functionality if you’re very uncertain of the likely.

Stability

All shared web hosting plans include a 45-day money-back and 99.9% uptime guarantees, which is great although quite standard among serious hosting vendors.

User-Friendliness

cPanel is standard for HostGator, and is a convenient interface to govern many aspects of your website. The quickInstall tool, available on all web hosting plans, you can create many types of website: blog, forum, CMS, wiki, photo gallery, E-commerce store, and so much more.

Functionalities

HostGator provides standard functionalities for their cheapest plans. These are one click WordPress installs, free SSL certificate, free Domain Hostgator also provides a website builder with 100s of templates, which makes it easy to set up the foundational structure of the page, freeing up more time for content creation. HostGator has free migration support in case you like to move your site from another hosting provider. In sum, the amount and quality of functionalities are good, although not extraordinary.

Support

Availability: The company provides 24/7/365 support by telephone, live chat and e-mail for all enquiries which is market leading Language: HostGator has support in English, Spanish, Portugese, Chinese, Turkish, Indonesean Test: We have tested the only support chat, with some in-depth questions. After we posted our question it took 2 minutes for the customer advisor to be connected. After that it took 4 minutes for the advisor to come back with a relevant answer - which seems to be quite adequate. It's worth stating that the advisor and myself got disconnected due to a glitch in their system, however, another advisor was immediately allocated and picked up the thread with my followup questions. For most of the questions, the advisor gave fast adequate answers - however, for one of the more technical questions it took about 30 mins for them to come back with an answer. Therefore, we will not give.
